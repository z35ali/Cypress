Cypress System

ABOUT US

Cypress System allows the citizens of Toronto to report problems they notice on the streets, and allows them to track the process of resolving the problem. Through the map provided on this website, a citizen can accurately pinpoint the exact location of where the problem occured, specify the type of problem, and subscribe to subsequent notifications about the resolution of the problem. Citizens are also able to consult the map in order to learn about the problems in a given area.
